Starborne Spy Report Converter Bot

>>> INSTALLATION <<<

  1. INVITE the Discord Bot - RECOMMENDED PERMISSIONS | LOWEST PERMISSIONS
  2. Make a discord channel with either spy-data or spy-report in its name

>>> USAGE <<<

  1. Copy your spy report from in game, then paste it into the box below
  2. It will generate and automatically copy your report code. Go to your spy-report channel, paste (Ctrl-V) in there, hit enter. Voila!

>>> COMMANDS <<<

  • Arrival Calculator
    > SP::time [current-time] eta [eta]
    In a 24h time format and HH:MM:SS format
